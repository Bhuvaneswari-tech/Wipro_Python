Challenge 2: Student Performance Tracker
Scenario:
A school wants to calculate the average marks of a student and 
determine if the student qualifies for a merit certificate.

Requirements & Logic:
Input: Marks in 3 subjects (float).
Calculation: average = (m1 + m2 + m3) / 3
Boolean: is_merit = average >= 85

Output: Display formatted result with average and 
merit eligibility.

Input:
Enter marks for Subject 1: 90
Enter marks for Subject 2: 87.5
Enter marks for Subject 3: 80

Output:
----- Student Performance Report -----
Average Marks : 85.83
Merit Eligible: True
