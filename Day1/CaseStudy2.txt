Challenge 4: Fitness App Calorie Tracker
Scenario:
A fitness app records calories consumed and burned. 
The goal is to calculate the net calorie balance and determine 
if the user has met their calorie deficit goal.

Requirements & Logic:
Input: Calories consumed (int) and calories burned (int).
Calculation: net_calories = consumed - burned
Boolean: goal_achieved = net_calories < 2000
Output: Display net calories and goal status.

Challenge 5: Ride-Sharing Fare Estimator
Scenario:
A ride-sharing company needs a Python script to calculate the 
fare based on distance and determine if the fare exceeds a premium 
ride threshold.

Requirements & Logic:
Input: Distance in km (float) and rate per km (float).
Calculation: fare = distance * rate
Boolean: is_premium = fare > 500
Output: Display fare and premium status.
